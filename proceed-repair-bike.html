How to repair a bicycle?

More than the meaning of  recycling,  seeking to know how to repair an old  bicycle  is a question of identity, nostalgia or heritage, in which the equipment can be bequeathed to a person who will continue to render good service to it.

How to repair an old bicycle?

First of all, a thorough check of all components must be carried out. This is not a simple test, but you have to disassemble the parts, focusing for example on the following order:

  • Wheels , including  tires,  air  hoses, spokes (  the   static wiring that connects the axle and the axle itself, plus the rear sprocket.
  • The brakes , both their wiring and the condition of the grip rubbers.
  • Pedals , starting from axle, arms, rubber and/or steel plates, central axle, rubbers, washers and special nuts.
  • The chain , which must be free of  rust , outdated or clogged links. If it does not feel flexible enough when removed, it should not be reused.
  • Condition of the  saddle , whether its  hydraulics  (to raise and lower it), pins, condition of the foam rubber padding, leather and its comfort (for health reasons , it is also important to know how to repair an old bicycle, because an uncomfortable seat can cause hernias or varicoceles).
  • Handlebar condition ,  twist ,  brake pressure, rubber grips.

However, performing all of this can be a daunting task, which is why it is best to entrust the  bike repair in Lyon  to a professional. As a result, your  bike  will not only be repaired but also overhauled and maintained.

Repair of an old bicycle step by step

The  bike  must be systematically dismantled. Preferably by stretching out and taking apart each area as you go. so that when  replacing parts or replace them depending on their condition. You know what each component does and how it does it. Always start by checking and painting the frame, as this is the most important part. Remove the rust and get help making new welds for reinforcement. This is about calling in a professional. Then paint it and check the condition of the axles and chain. After, install the rubbers and attach them to the chain. Check that they turn smoothly and that each tooth of the sprocket fits into the links. Wheels should spin freely, tested without riding,  spinning the bike .

The way to fix an old bike is to adapt it to your needs and make it safe. Connect the  handlebars  and  brakes  and repeat the previous step performing  brake tests.  If it does not brake easily, check the tires and cables and their pressure relative to the rim. Once the above has been positively verified. Place the seat and test it in the round on a flat place. Check that the seat doesn’t go down, the brakes stop, air isn’t leaking from the  tires,  the steering wheel steers smoothly, and the entire surface has no rough spots or  pitting.
