What is Glamping?

Glamping is the new form of vacation , for all travelers who love camping, but cannot do without the luxury and comfort of a hotel. Let’s discover the characteristics together.

Glamping: a popular style of accommodation

The new frontier of holidays and tourism is knocking at the door of those who love camping. But do not want to give up comfort and luxury. Sleeping bag and tent enemies will now have to think again. With the possibility of being outdoors but in a completely different way. Glamping is the new trend in travel , with a type of tourism that meets the desires of many travelers. Invest in this type of tourism development project  has therefore become a profitable investment for young entrepreneurs. This mode of camping has nothing to envy to hotels, but with an extraordinary view and the sound of nature that accompanies it throughout the day and night.

These unusual accommodations are tastefully furnished, the beds are king-size and there is always a well-stocked kitchen available. Amenities abound, such as a bathroom with jacuzzi, Wi-Fi and 5-star luxury.

But where does this strange term come from?

It is a neologism that has spread like wildfire. By combining Glamor  and  Camping , first used in Britain in 2005.

What are the types of glamping?

There are different types of luxury campsites . Some of them are unusual and atypical accommodations. These include in particular:

  • House in the trees:  realizing everyone’s dream and equipped with all the facilities of a 5-star hotel (extraordinary view included);
  • Eco Lodge:  a wooden structure with very low environmental impact that winks at nature without causing the tourist to miss anything;
  • The yurt:  a typical circular wooden structure covered with fabric, with a mixture of cotton and felt that stops time in distant times;
  • Safari tent:  an ecological wooden structure with a sloped roof and are now installed in various places;
  • Teepee: it is very reminiscent of the houses of American Indians. These accommodations are built with wooden logs arranged in a triangle. The roof is made of fabric, but the amenities are glamorous.

Today, there are many unusual forms and types of luxury camping accommodation. It is up to the entrepreneur to see what type of glamping meets the needs of tourists. To facilitate the realization of the project, it is important to seek help from an agency specializing in the development of tourism projects.
