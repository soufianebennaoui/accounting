Company domiciliation in Casablanca

Company domiciliation

ALARIS LAW FIRM offers you a strategic legal and fiscal address in the heart of Casablanca at a reasonable price. Domiciliating your company is imperative since it is one of the first steps in the constitution of a company and is within the reach of any person wishing to create his own company as opposed to the acquisition of a head office which requires important means. It is a beneficial strategy from an administrative and financial point of view, as it avoids countless costly and time-consuming commitments.

Entrust the domiciliation of your company to the specialist. Our goal is to satisfy you by providing you with services that meet your expectations.

Advantages of the domiciliation

You can choose the address of your company’s headquarters. This choice is important because the address of your company’s headquarters will determine the image and the first impression sent back by the latter to your customers and partners.

Domiciliation services allow you to have a perfect seal between the company address and your personal address. Depending on the nature of your business, it is possible that some of your customers or partners may want to go to your company’s headquarters for many reasons.

A domiciliation service allows you to avoid a change of address when you move, especially if you are a tenant. You save money and avoid numerous administrative procedures.

The domiciliation also allows you to protect the privacy of the legal representative of the company with the trade and companies register.
