How to choose the right hand shower?

The shower head, also called a shower head or hand shower . Is the device through which the water that we use to shower comes out. It remains connected to the bath tap by a hose or hose and can come in different shapes and sizes. He also releases water in different ways depending on his buds.

How to choose a shower head?

Wippot accessories  are an excellent investment, allowing you to reduce your water consumption without sacrificing comfort. It’s a set that meets both your water-saving projects and your family’s lifestyle needs. Water and energy savings don’t have to compromise a well-deserved relaxing shower at the end of the day. To find the best shower in terms of value for money and according to your needs. It is essential that you consider these factors:

  • Number and material of nozzles and holes: The best shower heads have over 200 holes of varying sizes. These should preferably be made of non-clogging silicone. So you don’t have to worry about lime clogging them.
  • Types of jets: rain effect, massage, high pressure, etc. ; the more, the better. In addition, it must have a selector on the head itself or on the structure of the shower head.
  • Materials: There are great deals on stainless steel showerheads, which are of the highest quality. The cheaper ones are made of ABS plastic. However, the pipe should always be made of a corrosion-resistant metal.
  • Pressure Boost: This feature is typical of showerheads recommended for homes where water comes out with little force. They are able to increase it by 20% to 40%, depending on the model.
  • Water Saving: Through the use of filters, diffusers and booster systems, many showerheads can save between 30% and 50% of water per shower.

What is a hand shower and how does it work?

The function of the hand showers is obviously to allow you to shower comfortably. They consist of the shower arm, the base of the structure and the part where you hold the showerhead to take a shower. The shower head marks the way the water comes out. This makes it possible, for example, to distinguish rain shower heads from conventional shower heads.

The limescale filter that retains up to 90% of limescale particles present in the water to make it cleaner for swimming. The pipe that connects the showerhead to the tub faucet through a series of joints. The shower rail allows you to hold the shower head in the right position for showering without having to hold it.

The operation of the best hand showers is very simple. Water comes to them through the tap and through the hose or garden hose. It goes through a limescale filter and comes out through the shower head according to the settings you have made. High pressure showerheads can be used to treat contractures and to perform massages. They are also very useful for cleaning the bathtub . The shower tray and, if the hose is long enough, the rest of the bathroom.
