How to repair your electric scooter?

The craze for personal mobility vehicles (PMVs) is undeniable. More and more users are moving at speeds between 6 and 25 km/h on all kinds of devices. Although some can go up to 30 km/h. Like any other chattel subject to rattling, these devices require periodic maintenance to perform their function.

Maintenance and main repairs of a scooter

The parts of the VMP that are most often affected by the  repair of electric scooters in Lyon   are those that do intense work when they are in operation. These are the wheels, steering and damping, brakes and throttle. And, of course, the driving force behind the device is the rechargeable electric battery , which also has its part to play .

Tires

The tires and wheels of these vehicles are subject to heavy wear. Since they have a very small radius and section, the number of cycles required. To walk a few meters is very high. This means that, despite being solid rubber tires , they are subject to frequent wear. And so, part of their periodic maintenance is to replace them withwheels. As fortires withinner tubes, such as those ofbicycles,motorcycles andcars, they also suffer punctures. Fixing them can be a real headache. If you’re not a handyman, replacing wheels will be a chore.

Accelerator and brakes

The scooter handle includes the throttle cable which draws power from the electric motor. This cable must be tensioned accordingly and without slackening so that it does not lose effectiveness and that the handlebar responds quickly to our hand. Vehicle brakes are usually disc brakes. This includes the caliper containing the brake pads and brake fluid which, like a car or motorcycle, needs to be replaced periodically.

Damping and steering

With all the imperfections of urban pavement , the steering is prone to rattle relative to the footpeg, which over time generates play. The same goes for the scooter ‘s suspension . If it has suffered severe shocks or the user is heavy, it may have a shorter lifespan, which will recommend its replacement with a new suspension.

Battery and electrical circuit

These elements must be replaced by professionals who, in addition to giving us advice on use, charging and recharging, dispose of the used battery at a clean point.

Advice before repairing an electric scooter

Scooter repair shops remain a technical support service, whether official or not. If you plan to leave your scooter to be repaired at a scooter repair center , know that it repairs your brand and its level of specialization. Some repairers only deal with last generation vehicles of a specific brand. And others repair all brands, even scooters that have been discontinued. Choose the one who is able to have the tools andspare parts for yourscooterand who knows the electronics of the most obsolete devices.

Also, remember that you are entitled to an advance estimate for the repair required. If the repair is very complex, the technician may ask you for money for the estimate only. However, he must inform you before you authorize the repair. Finally, you must be given an itemized invoice indicating labor and parts replaced, taxes and repair warranty in writing. The invoice must include all the data enabling the technical service and the user to be identified.
