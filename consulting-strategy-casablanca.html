Individual and group coaching in Casablanca

Coaching is a form of assistance offered to a person or a team through a one-time intervention or a long-term support. It aims to create the conditions that allow the person (or the team) to find and build their own solutions. This assistance places the resolution sought in a perspective of sustainable and global development. We accompany you in :

  • Collective coaching of your teams and your management bodies (Management Committee, Board of Directors)
  • Acting on the leadership posture and personal projection within the company (hierarchy, interest of the work, meaning)
  • Self-knowledge, personal and professional development
  • Ability to take risks and initiatives
  • Trust and self-confidence
  • Managing crises, managing pressure from the top down or from the bottom up
