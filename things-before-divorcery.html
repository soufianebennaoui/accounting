Things to do before divorce

Marriage can be defined as a legal union between two individuals. This is about bonding with the person you love for life. But this pact of love is often broken for several reasons such as infidelity, lack of attention… And the condition for legally breaking the bond is to have recourse to divorce. This means that both parties officially relinquish the rights and obligations associated with the marital union. Before taking steps before the divorce, many French people call on agencies specializing in the field .. Discover in the lines that follow 4 things you absolutely must do before divorcing.

Gather your documents

The divorce process is a tedious step. Before starting it, it is therefore essential to know the formalities to be carried out. In other words, you must ask yourself the following question: in the event of a  divorce, what to do first  ? We recommend in this type of situation that you seek the help of an agency specialized in the matter in order to  collect your documents .. Indeed, a lot of evidence is needed to initiate divorce proceedings. For example, it may be a question here of proving that you hold a property or your interest in the various assets that make up your estate. In order to facilitate their disclosure and ensure their accuracy, it is important to collect documents such as:

  • tax declarations;
  • tax notices;
  • deeds of purchase or sale.

Check your bank accounts

In the context of an impending divorce, one of the other important things to do before starting the procedure is to take into account  the maintenance of your bank account.. For personal accounts, we recommend that you ask the bank to cancel the power of attorney that you may have given to your spouse. The existing amount in these accounts is always guaranteed to be kept until the split. If, on the other hand, you had opened a joint account, you may have to request the closure of the account, or at least its separation, with the consent of your spouse. In addition, you must change the personal secret code, known of course by your former partner. Even if your level of agreement with your ex-spouse allows you to easily win your case following the proposal of these different measures,

Protect children’s property

Other precautions are also useful if you have minor children. You could thus ensure the protection of their heritage. It will suffice in this case to first make a  copy of your family record book , in order to be able to carry out the necessary steps for this purpose. Then, it will be a question of inventorying the personal effects of your child in parallel with your inventory of the goods.

Make an inventory

It is strongly recommended to  make an inventory  so that certain goods do not disappear before the sharing operation. This inventory can for example concern the  objects found in the family home.  And to carry out this operation, you can seek the help of an agency specializing in the field. Its major role here will be to draw up a complete list of your assets. It will also include in its service an estimate of the value of said goods.
