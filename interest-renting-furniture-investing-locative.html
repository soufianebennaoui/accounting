Taxation LMNP

Investor and future buyer, the status of Non-Professional Furnished Renter (LMNP) is an advantageous tax status . When you want to invest in stone for a rental project . What are the advantages of this status? What conditions must be met to benefit from it? The point to understand all the workings!

What is the point of renting furnished accommodation when investing for rental?

Unlike bare rental, furnished rental is considered a taxable commercial activity in the category of Industrial and Commercial Profits (BIC). Thus, in terms of taxation, the two rental methods do not have the same tax advantages.

The main tax characteristics in favor of furnished rentals:

  • Under the micro scheme, the fixed allowance is 20% higher for furnished rentals (50% for furnished rentals compared to 30% for bare rentals).
  • For furnished rentals, the ceiling for the micro scheme is much higher (€72,600 compared to €15,000 for bare rentals).
  • Another criterion that characterizes the furnished rental (at the real regime): the possibility of tax deducting the depreciation of the value of the property and the furniture from your rental income. As the amount of your taxable rental income is reduced, your tax is logically less substantial. This is a significant difference between bare rental and furnished rental since in bare rental  , this mechanism is not exploitable.

The conditions to be met to benefit from LMNP status

To take advantage of the status of Non-Professional Furnished Rental  Company (LMNP), and therefore of the  LMNP taxation , the rental income (if it is greater than €23,000) must be  less than or equal to the other income of the tax household.

In other words, you can very well claim LMNP status if you exceed the annual ceiling of €23,000 as long as your rental income remains below the income of the tax household. For example, generating €30,000 in rental income does not affect your eligibility for LMNP status if the tax household income is €60,000.

On the other hand, if you receive at least €23,000 in rental income from furnished rentals and this income represents more than half of your other income, you are then considered to be a Professional Furnished Rental Company (LMP). Without going into details, this means that you will pay social charges on your rental income and that you will depend on the professional capital gains regime.

LMNP taxation: all you need to know about this tax exemption mechanism

The chosen tax system can totally upset the calculation of the tax on the rents generated. In LMNP, two tax options are possible:  the micro-Bic regime and the real regime . Depending on which one you choose, the amount of your tax can change dramatically, which is why understanding LMNP taxation is key to saving tax.

The micro-Bic diet

To benefit from the micro-Bic scheme, your annual income must not exceed €72,600 in a classic furnished rental . And €172,600 for classified furnished tourist rentals and guest rooms.

Under the micro-Bic scheme, your tax is calculated on your rental income after an allowance:

  • 50% for  furnished rentals ,
  • 71% if you  have a classified « furnished accommodation » or for guest rooms .

The actual diet

The real regime is a tax regime that applies automatically if your annual income exceeds €72,600. On the other hand,  you can opt for the real plan even if your income is lower than this ceiling . And very often, this choice turns out to be much more suitable for saving tax.

At actual speed:

  • your expenses are deductible  : notary fees, maintenance and repair costs, local taxes (CFE, property tax, etc.), accounting fees, management fees, loan interest, etc.
  • depreciation of the value of the property and other depreciable expenses  are also deductible from your taxable income.

Typically, nearly 90% of investors who choose the real plan. Manage to effectively reduce the taxation of their rental income, or even to cancel it.

How to choose the most suitable tax regime for your real estate project?

No doubt, if the idea comes to you to invest in real estate and to favor LMNP status. Getting started without knowing the characteristics and limits of this status can be detrimental to you from a tax point of view. Why ?

In most cases, the micro-Bic regime is not the most favorable tax regime for saving tax. To know which diet to turn to, calculations are needed!

  1. Calculate the amount of your taxable income by deducting the allowance from your rental income ( micro-Bic regime).
  2. Calculate the amount of your taxable income by deducting your charges and depreciation from your rental income (actual regime).
  3. Then compare! With which calculation is your tax base the lowest?

To properly take into account all costs and calculate depreciation, the advice of a professional is recommended if you want to optimize your taxation in LMNP. This will also be the most able to inform you about the other characteristics of the LMNP status (deficit management, capital gain on resale, etc.).
