Rental of meeting rooms, training rooms and all types of spaces

The rental of event spaces is currently a very popular solution for professionals and individuals who wish to organize their events in a unique way. Event complexes exist at the moment to meet everyone’s needs.

If you are looking for a space to rent

Resident of Grenoble or passing through? You will easily have  rooms and spaces to rent by the hour  that you can  keep directly online . The  Alpexpo Alpes Congrès event center in Grenoble  is a good address for those looking for the perfect place for their various events . All room prices, whether  meeting rooms ,  seminar centers, convention spaces or other types of space are inclusive of VAT. You can always discuss the rates with the manager, clearly explaining your expectations. Everything depends on the  duration of the rental , the number of participants and the  type of room .

On the park’s website, you will find information on renting spaces . In the majority of cases, the meeting rooms installed in these types of complex are modular. The goal in fact is to be able to satisfy the needs of the tenants. Renting such a space during your specific events is advantageous, since it is often a question of receiving customers and audiences. It would be wiser for everything to take place in an unfamiliar environment.

What types of spaces can you find and how?

At Alpexpo, different types of rooms are available for hire, regardless of the activity to be carried out there. Do you want a  spacious place for your conferences , or to celebrate a birthday party? Are you looking for an  office for occasional meetings  or outside your usual place of work , are you planning to organize an exhibition or trade fair and you don’t know where?

At  the Alpexpo event complex  in Grenoble, you will come across what you really want. The price ranges suit all budgets. After having passed or chosen a service, it is quite possible to  become a member  and be one of the members who benefit from many advantages. Whether you are looking for a workplace to rent for a few hours or a space to carry out a specific activity, you can find it on the site and book it directly without availability problems, in a practical, dynamic and uncomplicated way.

In addition, members have access to the many outdoor activities available at the centre. Depending on the surface of the exhibition center, it can accommodate a whole crowd of visitors and guests. This is beneficial for companies preparing for an important event. Convention centers are also on site with different features.
